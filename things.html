Scenic Biking & Photos

Rent a bike and cycle along the scenic roads of South Batan to enjoy the iconic views of Racuh a Payaman (Marlboro Hills).

Useful Tips

The dry season (March to May) offers the best weather for touring, with clear skies and calm seas for island hopping. However, this is also peak season, so expect higher prices and more tourists.

Always practice honesty, respect the locals' privacy (especially when taking photos of their stone houses), and minimize littering. The Ivatans take great pride in their clean and safe environment.

It is highly recommended to book flights and accommodation months in advance, especially during peak season. Local tours (tricycle or van) can usually be arranged through your accommodation once you arrive, but booking ahead provides peace of mind.

Bike Rental & Safety

Biking is a popular way to explore Batan Island. Rental rates typically range from ₱300 to ₱500 per day.

Safety Tips:

  • The hills are steep; choose an e-bike or be prepared for a difficult workout.
  • Wear a helmet and use reflective gear.
  • The wind can be very strong—always maintain control!
