Welcome to Batanes!

Batanes is the northernmost province of the Philippines, famous for its scenic hills, stone houses, and peaceful atmosphere. It’s known as the “Home of the Winds” and loved for the honesty and kindness of its people, the Ivatans.

Top Attractions in Batanes

Discover the breathtaking spots that make Batanes a true paradise.

Basco Lighthouse

Basco Lighthouse is located on Naidi Hills in Basco, Batanes, overlooking the vast sea and green landscapes. The place is peaceful and scenic, perfect for watching sunsets and enjoying the cool breeze of the island.

House of Dakay

The House of Dakay is the oldest stone house in Batanes, built to withstand strong typhoons and earthquakes. Located in Ivana, it shows the traditional Ivatan way of living and architecture.

Mount Carmel Chapel

Mount Carmel Chapel, also known as Tukon Chapel, is a beautiful stone church on a hill in Batanes. It offers a peaceful atmosphere and a stunning view of the surrounding mountains and sea.

Racuh a Payaman

Racuh a Payaman, also known as Marlboro Hills, is one of the most scenic spots in Batanes. It features wide green pastures, rolling hills, and breathtaking views of the sea and mountains.

Vayang Rolling Hills

Vayang Rolling Hills is a famous spot in Batanes known for its endless green slopes and panoramic ocean views. The area is peaceful and breezy, perfect for sightseeing and relaxation.

Morong Beach and Nakabuang Arch

Morong Beach in Sabtang, Batanes is known for its white sand and clear blue waters. Its main attraction, the Nakabuang Arch, is a natural rock formation that beautifully frames the sea and sky.
