Basco Lighthouse

Location: Naidi Hills, Basco, Batanes

Short History: Built in 2003, the Basco Lighthouse stands as a symbol of safety and beauty, overlooking the Batanes coastlines.

How to Get There: Take a tricycle from Basco town proper (around 10 minutes).

Description: Perfect for sunset viewing, the lighthouse offers panoramic views of the sea, mountains, and nearby villages.

House of Dakay

Location: Ivana, Batanes

Short History: Built in 1887, it is the oldest stone house in Batanes still standing today.

How to Get There: A short tricycle or van ride from Basco to Ivana.

Description: The House of Dakay represents the resilience and traditional Ivatan culture.
